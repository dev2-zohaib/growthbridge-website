What leadership teams actually need from a marketing ROI dashboard

Dashboards should connect channel spend, funnel performance, and revenue impact—not just clicks and impressions.

The most effective dashboards answer three questions quickly: where demand is coming from, how efficiently it converts, and what should change next. GrowthBridge recommends a layered view that ties acquisition cost, pipeline creation, win rates, and revenue influence into one decision-ready summary.

For many companies, the biggest issue is not the dashboard itself—it is inconsistent tracking, mismatched lifecycle definitions, and delayed reporting. Cleaning the data model first creates the foundation for trustworthy executive insight.

When implemented well, ROI dashboards reduce wasted spend, improve budget allocation, and speed up performance reviews across teams.
