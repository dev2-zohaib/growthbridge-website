Fixing the handoff between lead capture and CRM follow-up

The time between inquiry and first response often determines whether a lead converts.

Many teams assume forms are working because submissions appear in a spreadsheet or inbox. But the real issue is what happens next: routing delays, incomplete enrichment, missing notifications, and inconsistent lifecycle triggers can quietly damage conversion performance.

GrowthBridge typically maps the entire lead handoff flow from form submission through CRM creation, ownership assignment, first outreach, and nurture enrollment. That exposes where response times slow down and where automation should intervene.

Even small workflow fixes can materially improve speed-to-lead and booked meeting rates.
