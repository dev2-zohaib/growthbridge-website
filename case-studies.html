Regional services

Lead generation acceleration with stronger local visibility and CRM workflows

A regional home services business struggled with inconsistent lead volume and slow follow-up. GrowthBridge overhauled local search visibility, connected form tracking to CRM routing, and deployed automated lead response sequences.

  • Average response time dropped from 4 hours to under 15 minutes
