Case studies

Examples of strategy and execution tied to measurable outcomes.

B2B SaaS

Challenge

A SaaS startup had healthy traffic but weak demo conversion rates and unclear attribution between paid media and sales-qualified opportunities.

Solution

GrowthBridge rebuilt landing page messaging, simplified form flows, aligned paid search intent with conversion paths, and connected campaign reporting to CRM stages.

Results

  • 42% increase in demo conversion rate
  • 31% lower cost per qualified opportunity
  • Shared KPI visibility for marketing and sales leadership

E-commerce

Challenge

An online retailer faced rising acquisition costs, inconsistent creative testing, and low retention from first-time buyers.

Solution

We restructured campaign architecture, tightened audience targeting, added lifecycle email segmentation, and implemented a blended performance dashboard.

Results

  • 24% lift in blended ROAS
  • 18% increase in repeat purchase rate
  • Faster decision-making using consolidated reporting

Regional service business

Challenge

A multi-location service company depended on referrals and had limited visibility into local search performance and website conversion behavior.

Solution

GrowthBridge improved local SEO, clarified service page messaging, optimized call-to-action placement, and streamlined lead follow-up workflows.

Results

  • 53% growth in qualified local inquiries
  • 22% increase in booked consultations
  • Improved speed-to-lead and reporting confidence
